🎯 WHAT YOU HAVE - QUICK OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A complete Android application that:

  ✅ Monitors clipboard in background (AccessibilityService)
  ✅ Automatically cleans URLs with regex patterns
  ✅ Removes tracking parameters (e.g., Instagram's ?igsh=...)
  ✅ Maintains history of all modifications
  ✅ Beautiful Material Design 3 interface
  ✅ Works completely offline (no internet needed)
  ✅ Stores everything locally (no cloud/tracking)

Example: Copy this URL to clipboard
  BEFORE: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DUOGuoeCnkN/?igsh=azF2ZGhkbzNueTUx
  AFTER:  https://www.instagram.com/reel/DUOGuoeCnkN/
  (Tracking parameter removed automatically!)
